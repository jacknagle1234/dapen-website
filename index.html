URGENT

One Click on Your Website Could Cost You $20,000+

Under the Americans with Disabilities Act, your website — just like your physical business — must be accessible to people with disabilities. If it’s not, you can be legally targeted.

In 2025, law firms are expected to hit over 250,000 small businesses with quick-settlement ADA website lawsuits. One demand letter could cost you thousands. The DAPEN® Defense Fund gives you full legal support the moment you’re targeted, protecting you from predatory firms and costly payouts.

Our Mission

DAPEN exists to protect small businesses from the growing threat of predatory ADA website lawsuits. We believe accessibility should be achieved through education, support, and awareness — not fear and punishment.

As accessibility standards evolve, we help businesses make a smooth transition by providing practical guidance, compliance tools, and affordable protection. Our mission is to create a web that works for everyone — without putting small business owners at risk.
